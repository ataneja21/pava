Elevated Ramen
Take your ramen to the next level in under 10 minutes.
Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions and drain.
While noodles are cooking, heat up oil in a separate pan.
In a heat-safe bowl, mix together ramen seasoning packet, soy sauce, minced garlic, noodle water, and chili flakes.
Pour heated oil over seasoning.
Mix cooked noodles with seasoning and serve with chopped green onion garnish.
Directions
Pack of your favorite ramen
Chili Flakes - 1 tsp
Minced Garlic - 1 tsp
