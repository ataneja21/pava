meals for college kids by college kids
Hi! We are four college students, Pramati, Alina, Vina, and Annaika (PAVA!). Our mission is to feed college students, either through quick recipes they can make or restaurants they can find nearby! We want to help students stay fed while juggling classes, extracurriculars, jobs, and anything else! You can look at the Gallery tab to see some pictures of recipes made. Otherwise, you can go to the Recipes tab, and find a recipe to recreate. Happy cooking!
