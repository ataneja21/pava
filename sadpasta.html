Sad Pasta
Overall, this is not pasta to make when you’re sad but pasta that just looks kinda sad.
Boil water, and toss the ravioli in. Let it cook for 5-6 minutes thoroughly and then drain the pasta water
Put the pasta and the entire jar of pasta sauce (yes, the entire jar) in a pan and simmer them together, adding spices as preferred
Now you have sad pasta, that looks really sad and tastes ok
Directions
One box of Trader Joe’s ravioli
One box of Trader Joe’s Pasta Sauce
